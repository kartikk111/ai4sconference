First International Workshop on AI for Geo-Spatial Data Innovation (AI4GSD)

Introduction

The fusion of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Geo-Spatial data presents an unprecedented opportunity to revolutionize various sectors, from urban planning to environmental conservation. Harnessing the power of AI in Geo-Spatial data analysis can lead to innovative solutions for addressing complex challenges and promoting sustainable development. This workshop aims to explore the synergy between AI and Geo-Spatial data, focusing on advancing innovative approaches for sustainable solutions.

Objective:

The primary objective of the AI for Geo-Spatial Data Innovation workshop is to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders in the field. The workshop aims to explore cutting-edge advancements, challenges, and opportunities in leveraging AI for the analysis and utilization of Geo-Spatial data for sustainable solutions.

Call for Papers

Researchers, practitioners, and students are invited to submit original research papers, case studies, and review articles on topics related to AI for Geo-Spatial data innovation. Contributions are solicited in areas including, but not limited to:

  • AI & Machine Learning for Sustainable Development Goals
  • Deep Learning for Sustainable Development
  • AI for Disaster Management & Sustainability
  • Architecture & Applications for Data Acquisition and Processing
  • Privacy, Security, and Social Implications on Acquiring Sustainability
  • Ontologies & Semantic Reasoning for Sustainable Development
  • Sustainable Development in Healthcare, Business, and Tourism
  • Efficient, Sustainable Technology for Smart Cities and Futures
  • Sustainable Development & E-Governance

Workshop Structure:

The workshop will feature keynote presentations, invited talks, paper presentations, panel discussions, and interactive sessions. Esteemed experts from academia, industry, and government agencies will share their insights and experiences, providing participants with valuable perspectives on the application of AI for Geo-Spatial data innovation.

Target Audience:

The workshop targets researchers, professionals, policymakers, and practitioners involved or interested in the intersection of AI and Geo-Spatial data. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in interdisciplinary discussions, network with peers, and explore collaborative opportunities for advancing sustainable solutions using AI-driven Geo-Spatial data analysis.
